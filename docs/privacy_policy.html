Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

At CropGuard AI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to provide and improve our service:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, synchronization across devices, and secure authentication via Firebase.
  • Crash Data: Anonymous technical logs (via Firebase Crashlytics) to help us identify and fix app crashes.
  • FCM Tokens: Unique identifiers used to send you notifications about crop health and app updates.

2. Image Data (On-Device Processing)

Your crop scan images are processed entirely on your device. We use an offline TensorFlow Lite model to analyze your leaf photos. We do NOT automatically upload your photos to our servers. Your photos stay on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them with the community or for support purposes.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including Firebase's secure infrastructure, to protect your authenticated data. We do not sell or share your personal information with third-party advertisers.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time. You can delete your account directly within the app settings or by contacting us.
